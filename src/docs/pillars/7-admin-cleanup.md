# Admin Cleanup

**Priority: üî¥ MANDATORY**

## Overview

Essential administrative updates including **PAN, Aadhaar, and KYC** are mandatory upon returning to India. Failure to complete these updates can result in inoperative accounts, blocked investments, and inability to access financial services.

## Key Action

> **Update PAN to Resident, obtain/update Aadhaar, link both**

## Why Admin Cleanup is Mandatory

When you return to India as a resident, your entire financial identity must reflect this change:

| Issue if Not Updated | Consequence |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Inoperative PAN | Higher TDS (20%), cannot file ITR |
| Unlinked PAN-Aadhaar | PAN becomes inoperative |
| NRI status in MF/Demat | Transactions blocked |
| Outdated bank KYC | Account restrictions |

---

## Part 1: PAN Card Updates

### Why Update PAN Status

Your PAN (Permanent Account Number) is linked to your residential status. Banks, mutual funds, and tax authorities check this status.

### Changing Status from NRI to Resident

#### Method 1: Through Income Tax Portal
1. Log in to [incometax.gov.in](https://incometax.gov.in)
2. Go to **My Profile** ‚Üí **Personal Details**
3. Update residential status to "Resident"
4. Submit and verify

#### Method 2: Through Form 49A (if updating other details)
1. Download Form 49A from NSDL website
2. Check "Correction" box
3. Update address and status
4. Submit with supporting documents

### Required Documents
- Valid Indian address proof
- Passport copy
- Recent photograph

---

## Part 2: Aadhaar Card

### For Returning NRIs

**Good News**: NRIs with valid Indian passport can apply for Aadhaar at any Aadhaar Seva Kendra. The previous **182-day residency requirement has been waived** for NRIs holding valid Indian passports.

### Who Can Apply

| Type | Eligibility |
|------|------------|
| **NRI with Indian Passport** | Can apply immediately upon arrival |
| **OCI Card Holder** | Must meet 182-day residency requirement |
| **PIO** | Must meet 182-day residency requirement |

### How to Apply

#### Step 1: Book Appointment
- Visit [uidai.gov.in](https://uidai.gov.in)
- Find nearest Aadhaar Seva Kendra
- Book appointment (walk-ins may have long waits)

#### Step 2: Visit Enrollment Center
Bring:
- Valid Indian passport (original + copy)
- Proof of Indian address
- Indian mobile number
- Email address

#### Step 3: Biometric Enrollment
- Fingerprints (10 fingers)
- Iris scan (both eyes)
- Photograph

#### Step 4: Receive Aadhaar
- EID (Enrollment ID) provided immediately
- Aadhaar number generated in 2-3 weeks
- mAadhaar app provides digital copy

### Updating Existing Aadhaar

If you already have Aadhaar but need to update address:

#### Online Update (Self-Service)
1. Visit [uidai.gov.in](https://uidai.gov.in)
2. **My Aadhaar** ‚Üí **Update Address**
3. OTP verification
4. Upload address proof

#### Offline Update
1. Visit Aadhaar Seva Kendra
2. Submit address update form
3. Provide new address proof

## Part 3: PAN-Aadhaar Linking

### Why It's Mandatory

| If Not Linked | Consequence |
|---------------|-------------|
| **PAN becomes inoperative** | Cannot use for financial transactions |
| **Higher TDS** | 20% instead of applicable rate |
| **ITR Issues** | Cannot file income tax returns |
| **Investment freeze** | Mutual funds, stocks blocked |
| **Bank restrictions** | New accounts may be rejected |

### NRI Linking Requirements

| Scenario | Linking Required? |
|----------|------------------|
| NRI (status correctly marked in PAN records) | ‚ùå Not mandatory |
| NRI who already has Aadhaar | ‚úÖ Must link to avoid inoperative PAN |
| **Returning NRI (becoming Resident)** | ‚úÖ **Mandatory** |

### How to Link

#### Online Method
1. Visit [incometax.gov.in](https://incometax.gov.in)
2. **Quick Links** ‚Üí **Link Aadhaar**
3. Enter PAN and Aadhaar numbers
4. Pay fee (‚Çπ1,000 if linking after deadline)
5. OTP verification via mobile linked to Aadhaar

#### SMS Method
Send SMS to 567678 or 56161:
```
UIDPAN <12-digit Aadhaar> <10-digit PAN>
```

### Fee for Late Linking
- **‚Çπ1,000** fee applies for linking after the original deadline
- Can be paid via [incometax.gov.in](https://incometax.gov.in) ‚Üí e-Pay Tax ‚Üí Challan 280

## Part 4: Bank Account KYC Update

### What to Update

| Account Type | Required Updates |
|--------------|------------------|
| NRE Account | Convert to Resident Savings |
| NRO Account | Convert to Resident Savings or close |
| RFC Account | Update address if applicable |
| All Accounts | Update Indian address, status, mobile |

### Documents Required

1. ‚úÖ Passport with arrival stamps
2. ‚úÖ New Indian address proof
3. ‚úÖ Updated PAN (showing Resident status)
4. ‚úÖ Aadhaar Card
5. ‚úÖ Recent passport-sized photograph
6. ‚úÖ Self-declaration of status change
7. ‚úÖ FATCA/CRS declaration

### Process

1. Visit your bank branch
2. Submit KYC update request
3. Provide all documents
4. Sign new declaration forms
5. Receive confirmation of update

### Re-KYC Requirements

Banks periodically require Re-KYC. Upon return:
- Complete Re-KYC for all accounts
- Update mobile number to Indian number
- Update email if needed

## Part 5: Investment Account Updates

### Mutual Funds

#### KYC Registration Agency (KRA) Update
1. Contact your KRA (CAMS, Karvy, etc.)
2. Submit KYC Modification Form
3. Update status from NRI to Resident
4. Link to new resident bank account

#### AMC Notification
1. Write to each AMC (Asset Management Company)
2. Submit status change form
3. Update bank mandate
4. Provide new KYC documents

### Demat Account

NRI Demat accounts must be converted to Resident Demat accounts.

#### Process
1. **Close NRI Demat**: Submit closure request to broker
2. **Open Resident Demat**: Apply for new resident account
3. **Transfer Securities**: Move holdings from old to new account
4. **Update Linked Account**: Link new resident bank account

#### Timeline
- Allow 2-3 weeks for complete transfer
- Some brokers offer internal conversion (simpler)

### Trading Account

- Update status from NRI to Resident
- New T+1 settlement rules apply (vs. PINS for NRIs)
- May get access to previously restricted products (Intraday, F&O)

---

## Part 6: Other Important Updates

### Power of Attorney (POA)
- Review and revoke any POAs given for NRI accounts
- Issue new POAs if needed for Indian accounts
- Update nominee details across all accounts

### Credit Cards
- Update address on Indian credit cards
- Consider getting new Indian credit card for easier billing
- Link to Indian bank account for autopay

### Loans (if any)
- Update address with lenders
- Ensure EMI mandates are on resident accounts

### Insurance Policies
- Update address with all insurance companies
- Convert any NRI policies if applicable
- Ensure premiums are paid from resident accounts

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

### Inoperative PAN

| Impact | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Higher TDS** | 20% deducted instead of applicable rate |
| **Cannot file ITR** | Returns rejected |
| **New investments blocked** | Cannot open new MF folios, demat accounts |
| **Loan applications rejected** | Banks require active PAN |

### Frozen Accounts

| Impact | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Transactions blocked** | Cannot withdraw, transfer, or invest |
| **SIPs stopped** | Systematic investments fail |
| **Salary credit issues** | Employer may face problems depositing salary |

## Rectifying Inoperative PAN

If your PAN became inoperative due to non-linking:

### Option 1: Online Request
1. Visit [incometax.gov.in](https://incometax.gov.in)
2. Download and complete **Aadhaar-PAN Link Request Form**
3. Pay ‚Çπ1,000 fee
4. Link Aadhaar
5. PAN reactivated in 7-30 days

### Option 2: Request to Jurisdictional Assessing Officer (JAO)
1. Submit request with:
   - PAN copy
   - Passport with visa stamps
   - Proof of overseas residence (if updating to NRI status first)
2. JAO updates records
3. Then link Aadhaar
